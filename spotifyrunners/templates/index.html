Spotify Runners

We make it easy to play your favorite music to keep you going on your run, without distracting you or throwing you off your pace. To get started, just enter a desired BPM and click the button below, and we'll create a playlist of your favorite songs that match it.

How it works

Our app looks at your favorite songs from your Spotify library and analyzes each one to determine its BPM, or Beats Per Minute. If a song's tempo matches your running pace, it won't distract you and can help you keep pushing.
